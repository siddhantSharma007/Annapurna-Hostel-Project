About Annapurna

Annapurna Hostel, Mathura is a boutique hostel with modern amenities and exemplary services, located about 250 meters from GLA University,Mathura. built and elegantly furnished, it offers esteemed Students a blend of luxury and comfort. Annapurna Hostel provide Cozy and comfortable accommodation for a pleasant stay.

We invite you to take a closer look and discover unmistakable hospitality in this hostel in Mathura that we call home.

Modern Amenities

We believe that a seamless living experience goes hand in hand with modern amenities. With high-speed internet access, study areas, and recreational spaces, we've got you covered for both work and play. Your safety and security are of utmost importance to us, and we maintain round-the-clock surveillance to ensure your peace of mind..

Homely Meals

They say the way to a happy heart is through a happy stomach, and we couldn't agree more! At Annapurna Hostel, we understand the significance of nutritious and wholesome meals in your daily life. Our in-house kitchen prepares a delectable assortment of dishes that cater to diverse tastes and dietary preferences. Enjoy home-cooked meals that not only satisfy your cravings but also provide you with the energy to conquer the day's challenges

Community and Engagement

Living away from family and friends can be daunting, but at Annapurna Hostel, you'll find a supportive and friendly community to lean on. From organized events and activities to common areas where you can socialize, our aim is to create an atmosphere that fosters connections and friendships. Our staff is dedicated to ensuring that you have all the support you need during your stay, making your experience at Annapurna truly memorable.
